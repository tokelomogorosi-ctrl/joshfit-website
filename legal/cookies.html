Cookies Notice

Last updated 1 June 2026

This notice covers cookies and similar technologies used on the Joshfit Personal Training website. It is published under the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations 2003 (PECR) and the UK GDPR.

The short version

The Joshfit website does not set any first-party cookies for tracking. The few third-party scripts the site loads (Google Fonts for typography, Plyr for video and audio playback, AOS for scroll animations) do not store personal data or build a behavioural profile on you. The site uses no marketing cookies, no advertising trackers, and no analytics that follow you to other sites.

What is loaded from third parties

Service What it does Cookies set?
Google Fonts Loads the Barlow Condensed and Inter typefaces from Google's CDN. No cookies. Logs your IP address briefly to serve the font.
Tailwind CDN (jsdelivr) Loads the Tailwind CSS framework. No cookies. Logs your IP address briefly to serve the file.
Plyr (cdn.plyr.io) Renders the video and audio players. Sets local storage to remember player volume preferences. Not a tracking cookie.
AOS (unpkg.com) Triggers scroll-reveal animations. No cookies.
Stripe Used only when you click the payment link. The Stripe page itself sets fraud-prevention cookies. Yes, but only on the Stripe payment page, never on this site.

How to clear or block

Most browsers let you clear local storage and cookies from settings. Blocking third-party scripts at the browser level will stop fonts from loading and the video and audio players from working, but the rest of the site will still function.

Future changes

If analytics or any other cookie-setting tool is added later, a consent banner will appear on first visit and this notice will be updated.
